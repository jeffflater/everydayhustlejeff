Pro Tips for Maximizing Earnings

Strategies I've learned from experience to help you earn more per hour.

Work Peak Hours

Lunch (11am-1pm) and dinner (5pm-9pm) are the busiest. Weekends are typically better than weekdays.

Know Your Zones

Learn which areas have the most restaurants and orders. Stay in busy zones to minimize downtime.

Multi-App Strategy

Sign up for multiple apps and switch between them to always have orders coming in.

Track Your Expenses

Gas, maintenance, and phone costs are deductible. Track everything to maximize your tax savings.
